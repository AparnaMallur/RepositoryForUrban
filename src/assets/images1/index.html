Why UrbanCompany

Urban Company (Formerly UrbanClap) was launched in Nov 2014. It is the largest home services platform in India and UAE, with recent expansion to Australia and Singapore. The platform helps customers book reliable home services – from beauty services to massage therapy, appliance repair to plumbing etc. Since its inception in November 2014, Urban Company has delivered 14 million service orders across 22 cities through a fleet of nearly 30,000 service professionals and has raised $215m in capital from marquee investors such Accel Partners, SAIF Partners, VY Capital and Ratan Tata to name a few.
